Whipped coffee rage started on TikTok where it originally got viral and now its all over. We wanted to test it out and see what the rage is all about and wow is it good! It only needs 3 ingredients to make with equal parts of instant coffee, granulated sugar, and hot or boiling water. We have made this Whipped coffee recipe using both granulated sugar and tried a healthier version using maple syrup to see what the difference is. Using granulated sugar will make the whipped coffee froth lighter in color and stays fluffy longer and even for hours. The fluffy coffee does dissolve quicker using maple syrup and would have to be served immediately but it is a healthier alternative to using sugar.

Ingredients




Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, add the instant coffee, Sugar (we used maple syrup), vanilla and hot/boiling water.
  2. Whisk on medium-high for 5-8 minutes, or until super fluffy and caramel looking.
  3. To assemble the iced coffee, add ice and milk of choice to a cup.
  4. Top with whipped coffee and serve immediately. Enjoy!
