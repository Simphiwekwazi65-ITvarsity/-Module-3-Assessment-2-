Shrimp-Fajita-Bowl

Prep time: 59 mins


Serving size: 4 servings

Yield: 4


R75


This Shrimp Fajita Bowl is so filling, easy to make, and made with cilantro lime cauliflower rice instead of rice for a low carb option. Loaded with shrimp and the perfect for meal prepping. They are a great option for a quick lunch or dinner.

delicious family meal

Ingredients









Steps

  1. Make the cauliflower rice – First, pulse the cauliflower in a food processor until it’s a rice consistency. Cook the cauliflower rice in a pan with some oil for 4-6 mins then season and set aside.
  2. Cook the shrimp fajita mix – Next, in a large pan with olive oil cook the veggies until softened, then cook the seasoned shrimp.
  3. Assemble the bowl – Then, divide the rice and shrimp fajita mix into bowls, top with beans, and avocado.
  4. Finally, serve and enjoy!
